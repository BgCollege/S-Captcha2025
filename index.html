S-CAPTCHA 2025

Student's Competition and Platform for Technological Challenges & Applications

5th & 6th February 2025

Guidelines For S-Captcha - 2025

  • Registration shall begin at 9.00 a.m. till half hour before the event.
  • All participants will have to bring their identity cards while reporting for the competition.
  • The different events will be assessed by a panel of judges
  • The decision given by judges in all matters will be final.
  • Traveling expenses will be borne by the students themselves or by their institutions.
  • Participants reporting late may not be allowed to participate in the competition.
  • Entry fee is not refundable.

Events & Competitions

Day 1 (5th Feb 2025)

Kaun Banega Tech Guru?

Code with Logic, Think with Vision!

Time: 11:00 AM to 1:00 PM

Rules :

  • Only Single student can participate in the competition.
  • The student should have knowledge of ‘C’ language and Data Structure.
  • Shortlisted participants of Round I will appear for Round II.
  • The Time duration 30min max.

Web Page Designing

Design. Develop. Dominate!

Time: 1:30 PM to 3:00 PM

Rules :

  • The maximum Two student can participate in the competition.
  • Designing should be done in HTML,CSS, Bootstrap.
  • The Participants has to produced minimum 3 pages of website.
  • Web Page Design Theme :- Business Category.
  • Website should include Home page, About Us, Contact Us along with Registration form and its validation.
  • No use of pre-designed templates.
  • All work must be done during the competition.
  • Internet usage is allowed.

Bug Tracker

Hunt the Bug, Rule the Code!

Time: 3:00 PM to 4:00 PM

Rules :

  • Competition is in “C language”.
  • Only one participant is allowed per entry.
  • Shortlisted participants of Round I will appear for Round II.

Day 2 (6th Feb 2025)

Treasure Hunt

Rise and Shine. It is hunting time!

Time: 10:00 AM to 12:00 PM

Rules :

  • Student Must Have participated as a team of maximum 4 students.
  • Participants should follow instruction give by instructor.
  • The Treasure Hunt consists of five clues, each one leading to the next clue.
  • Decisions taken by the judges will be treated as fair and final.

Poster Competition

Design. Inspire. Impact!

Time: 1:00 PM to 2:00 PM

Topics

  • Cyber Security
  • Block Chain
  • Advanced Mobile Technology and Trends
  • Artificial Intelligence

Rules :

  • Poster should be made by the participants on the given topics using their own creativity and innovation.
  • Poster can be accepted in soft copy or hard copy.
  • Maximum two participants for each poster are allowed.
  • The Time duration for poster presentation will be 5min max.

Project Competition

Showcase Creativity, Build the Future!

Time: 1:00 PM to 2:00 PM

Rules :

  • Student may participate in the competition as an individual or as a team of maximum 2 students.
  • A student is not allowed to participate in more than one project.
  • Project will be evaluated based on its innovation, creativity, technical expertise and presentation skills.
  • The Time duration for presentation will be 7 to 10 min max.

Note :

  • Prize Ceremony will be conducted on 6th Feb 2025 2:00 PM Onwards.
  • Violation of rules by a participant will be considered as disqualification.
  • Terms and Condition Applied.
